The Woman and the Mountain

I'm aware of you and somehow, to an extent, you are aware of me. By you, dear reader, I mean someone else. In fact, she turns from me and resumes her trek as I address you instead. Perhaps I should enlighten your expectant imaginations. The woman I was talking to is young in both looks and manner but there is no wondering of the capacity she has for thought and reflection for as we watch her, she has none other than that which is shared with me.
